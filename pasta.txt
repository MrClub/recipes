10 grams of salt cooks 100 grams of pasta in 1000 ml of water.
10-100-1000 rule

10 grams of salt is roughly equivalent to two teaspoons.
Method:
Large pan of water to roiling boil. Add salt. Add Pasta. Stir. Bring back to boil. Cook until al dente. Drain while reserving a cup. Do not pour cold water on pasta.
